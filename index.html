THE FIRM

A Legacy of Justice

Advocate Sampatlal & Sanjay Bohara have been pillars of the legal community in Udaipur for over five decades. Our firm is known for its unshakeable integrity and the deep relationships we have built through decades of dedicated service in Udaipur.

"Our experience in the field is strengthened by our loyalty to clients and commitment to upholding the law. We work closely with each client, understanding their needs and crafting a legal strategy that aligns with their goals."

We possess deep expertise in the workings of the Rajasthan High Court and the Ajmer Revenue Board.

LEADERSHIP

Partners & Counsel

Mr. Sampat Lal Ji Bohara

Founder & Senior Counsel

A Titan in the legal fraternity of Rajasthan, Mr. Sampat Lal Ji Bohara began his practice in 1969. Over 55 illustrious years, he has successfully resolved lakhs of complex civil and revenue disputes. Known for his eidetic memory of case law and compassionate approach, he is not just a lawyer but a mentor to dozens of advocates in Udaipur. His name is synonymous with integrity and victory in the District Courts.

EST. 1969 CIVIL LAW EXPERT

Mr. Sanjay Bohara

Co-Founder & Partner

Bringing modern legal dynamism to the firm, Mr. Sanjay Bohara has been practicing since 2002. He is a Gold Medalist in his LLB and holds a Master's (LLM). As a former President of the Law College, he has deep connections in the judiciary and administration. He specializes in high-stakes High Court appeals, complex Revenue Board matters, and defending NRI property rights.
