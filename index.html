THE FIRM

A Legacy of Justice in Rajasthan

Advocate Sampatlal & Sanjay Bohara have been pillars of the legal community in Udaipur for over five decades. Our firm is known for its integrity, and we are proud of the relationships we have built over decades of service in Udaipur.

"Our experience in the field is strengthened by our loyalty to clients and commitment to upholding the law. We work closely with each client, understanding their needs and crafting a legal strategy that aligns with their goals."

We possess deep expertise in the workings of the Rajasthan High Court and the Ajmer Revenue Board, ensuring that whether you are a local resident or an NRI, your property and civil rights are vigorously defended.

LEADERSHIP

Partners & Counsel

Mr. Sampat Lal Ji Bohara

Founder & Senior Counsel

A Titan in the legal fraternity of Rajasthan, Mr. Sampat Lal Ji Bohara began his practice in 1969. Known for his eidetic memory of case law and compassionate approach, he is a mentor to dozens of advocates in Udaipur.

Mr. Sanjay Bohara

Co-Founder & Partner

Practicing since 2002, Mr. Sanjay Bohara combines academic brilliance (LLM Gold Medalist) with courtroom aggression. As a former President of the Law College, he specializes in high-stakes Revenue Board matters.
