HAVE QUESTIONS?

Expert Answers

How do I book a consultation?
You can book a consultation by calling us directly at +91 94141 66841 or sending us a message on WhatsApp. We offer both in-person meetings at our Udaipur office and virtual consultations for out-of-town clients.
Do you handle cases in the Rajasthan High Court?
Yes, we have extensive experience representing clients in the Rajasthan High Court (Jodhpur & Jaipur benches) as well as the Board of Revenue in Ajmer.
Can you manage my property dispute if I live abroad?
Absolutely. Our specialized NRI Wing is designed for this exact purpose. Through a Power of Attorney (POA), we can manage the entire litigation process without requiring you to travel to India.
What documents should I bring for the first meeting?
Please bring all relevant property deeds, previous court orders, notices received, and a chronological summary of events. Digital copies are acceptable for virtual consultations.
How long does a typical civil suit take?
While timelines vary based on court procedures, we focus on expedited resolution strategies like injunctions and out-of-court settlements where possible to save you years of litigation.
